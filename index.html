Making of the website

During the making of this website I encountred many new obsticeles. Many were simply fixed by trail and error like most issues. However there were few isssues that had me troubling in my nighmares. On top of my head there were two main problems I encountred were:

  • Equal height columns
  • Responsive tables

Equal height columns

During the making of my website I encountered the problem of producing equal height columns, in my case getting the sidebar (aside) to be the same as the main. I searched the web to find many different solutions but the best that suited be was as provided here, Equal height columns.In this solution you add a bottom padding of an extreme value and negate the bottom margin of the same value, and allowing the containers overflow to be hidden. Simple as. However in the future flex box might be the better solution as it become supported widely.

Responsive tables

I had tables in my website and they seemed to break at a certain point on small screens, thus I though of making responsive tables. After a great deal of research on my part I discovered it was a difficult problem to solve. There are many solutions but not perfect and neither is the one is used, here is the full article Responsive tables. I adapoted the concept and use it to suit my needs, However during the process I noticed this solution only works for table that display information in columns (HTML - the set of information is on the same row). I hope to find a solution to this some time in the future.
